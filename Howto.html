WHAT ARE THE FEES AND TAXES FOR SELLING ON BOBLA?

BOBLA charges 5% on the total item price, which includes any shipping and gift wrapping costs that you have charged the customer.

HOW DO I OPEN A BOBLA SHOP?

After filling out a vendor application, a BOBLA rep will contact you via email to schedule a video interview. This is a quality control method that we've implemented to protect our users and our brand. If our missions align, our rep will send you a seller handbook with your login information to access the seller panel. 

You will find everything you need to get started in the seller handbook.

HOW DO I GET PAID ON BOBLA?

WHAT DO I DO AFTER I SELL AN ITEM?

After you make a sale on BOBLA, you can find all of the order details in your account. BOBLA also sends a notification to the BOBLA carrier app (for local deliveries) and an email to you at the address you use for your BOBLA account. You may want to add transaction@bobla.io to your contacts, so these emails don't go to your spam folder.

WHAT ABOUT SHOP POLICIES?

BOBLA sellers set their own policies because it's your shop, so your rules. Remember to list out all your shop policies such as shipping, returns/exchanges, payments, damaged items, customized items, and any other important guidelines you may think your customers need to know.
